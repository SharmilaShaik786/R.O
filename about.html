About Us

Welcome to SVR Water Solutions
Founded in 2007, SVR Water Solutions has evolved from a small company into a leading name in the global water industry. We specialize in providing high-quality water purification solutions, offering services like installation, maintenance, and 24/7 customer support to ensure clean and safe drinking water. Our product range includes RO components such as booster pumps, membranes, filters, housing, and accessories. We also manufacture industrial RO plants ranging from 50 LPH to 50,000 LPH, establishing ourselves as a trusted competitor in the global market.
